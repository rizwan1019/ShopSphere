Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShopSphere. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled safely and responsibly.

When you use our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number) when you contact us or sign up.
  • Payment information when making a purchase.
  • Browsing data, including IP address and device information.

We use the collected data to:

  • Process transactions and provide customer support.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share it with:

5. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and protect your credentials.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
